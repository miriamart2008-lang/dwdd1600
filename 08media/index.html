About the Author

Miriam Jones

My name is Miriam Jones and I was born in Spanish Fork, Utah. In my early years I loved art and reading which would become a refuge when my parents got divorced when I was young. Reading brought me to worlds where happily ever afters still existed, allowing me to believe that one day I would get a happily ever after too. Reading was still my refuge from the world but I started to create my own worlds and write short stories when I started Highschool. These experiences encouraged me to apply for the English Sterling Scholar scholarship. I became the English Sterling Scholar for my high school. The older I get the more my imagination goes wild and the more excited I am to write down my personal stories.
